History of Maracas

No one knows exactly when and where maracas were invented. We do know that they came from the native tribes of Latin America. They would take fruit from a Calabash tree, extract the pulp inside, and then place seeds or pebbles inside the fruit's shell. At Shaky Shaky Maracas, we hand carve our maracas from wood. This makes them extra strong and produces a much higher quality sound. Maracas are still a popular part of Latin American music today. What makes maracas so special is that nearly everyone can play them, which is why they are often a young child's first toy. Maracas are one of the most underrated instruments on the market today, and Shaky Shaky Maracas would be honored to become your premier maraca supplier.
