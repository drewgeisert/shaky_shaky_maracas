About Us

Shaky Shaky Maracas was founded on August 31st, 2017 by Karson Richardson and Drew Geisert. The company is based in the heart of America: Indianola, Iowa

Karson and Drew are students at Simpson College who are looking for a start in the business world by selling a unique and exquisite product. The idea of starting a business did not occur to the two ambitious men until they finished making their first batch of hand made maracas, which gained a lot of popularity in their hometown of Indianola, Iowa. They opened their first official Shaky Shaky Maracas store in Indianola on March 12th, 2017, in which they kept and sold their hand made maracas.

Josh was added onto the project to try to clean up Shaky Shaky Maracas' web presence. He did a lot of the styling that you see on the site and even added some content (this). He joined the project on September 19, 2017 and has been working hard ever since.

What are Shaky Shaky Maracas?

Shaky Shaky Maracas offers a brand new line of custom maracas for your entertainment. Each maraca is crafted to perfection by a team of men and women and before being hand painted by many of our fine artists. Our maraca's bright colors make playing the maracas fun for all ages.
