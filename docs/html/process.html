How Our Maracas are Made

  1. Wood is harvested from a local Iowan Ironwood Tree Farm
  2. The harvested wood is transported to our warehouse where it is cut into blocks
  3. The wood blocks are then carved into the shape of a maraca
  4. The maracas are then filled with fresh beans after being cut in half, and then glued back together
  5. After being glued back together, the maracas are hand painted by our professional artists
  6. The shaped maracas are sanded and shipped off to one of our many store locations
